The Initiative

Humane Quotient is a volunteer based service started by a young group of friends. With an aim to tackle the problem of laziness and ignorance towards donation, we provide service to pick up the donation from doorstep and make sure it reaches to the people who are in need the most or to the right organization who can take care of distributing it.

We are an ever growing group of people who want to change the world for better by extending a helping hand and bringing the joy of giving to the people.

The Idea

The idea is simple. You can donate items like clothes, blankets, stationery, utensils or other such items of daily use and necessity by simply filling up the donation form on our website. We will get in touch with you to schedule a pick up of the donation from your doorstep over the weekends.

You can also volunteer for a couple of hours on your weekend to be a part of this amazing initiative by filling up the volunteer form.
